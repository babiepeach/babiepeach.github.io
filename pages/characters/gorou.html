Gorou

| | |

May 18th | Canis Bellatoris

BASIC INFO:

"General Gorou of the Watatsumi Island resistance, ready and waiting! I've long heard of your sterling reputation and I look forward to fighting our way to victory side-by-side!"

Gorou is a resident of Inazuma, on Watatsumi Island, and is the general for the Watatsumi Resistence.

He yearns to be taller and stronger, which is a testiment to his dedication as a soldier, yet he is often very brash and let's the adrenaline of a battle to get to his head.
