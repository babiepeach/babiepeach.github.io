September 13th | Princeps Cretaceus

BASIC INFO:

"I am Albedo, Chief Alchemist of the Knights of Favonius. You carry the aura of the stars, interesting... I would like to study you, if you do not mind."

Albedo is an Alchemist based in Mondstat while working mainly in Dragonspine, currently working for the Knights of Favonius.

He spends most of his time alone in the harsh cold of the mountains, where he does his experiments and sketches as a form of relaxation.
