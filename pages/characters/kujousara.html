Kujou Sara

| | |

July 14th | Flabellum

BASIC INFO:

"I am Kujou Sara. You may forget my name, but this you must know: On my watch, Inazuma will always be defended, and the will of the Shogun will always be done."

Kujou Sara serves the Vision Hunt Decree in Inazuma, and is an adopted daught to the Kujou Clan.

She is someone who excells at battles, but ignores rematches, at the dismay to many.
