Qiqi

| | |

March 3rd | Pristina Nola

BASIC INFO:

"I am Qiqi. I am a zombie. And I forgot what comes next."

Qiqi is an apprentice and herb gatherer at Bubu Pharmancy in Liyue Harbor.

She is a zombie, and like other children, has really poor memory, so she writes as much down as she can in her notebook to not forget anything.
