Shenhe

| | |

March 10th | Crista Doloris

BASIC INFO:

"My name is Shenhe. I was born fated to bear the curse of calamity, so I am prone to bring harm to the people around me. The adepti urged me to bind my soul with red ropes to keep my homicidal nature at bay."

Shenhe is an adepti disciple from Liyue Harbour, who usually resides in the mountains.

She doesn't show much emotion due to the r4ed ropes constrained to her heart because of her abandonment when she was a child.
