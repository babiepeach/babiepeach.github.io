"I'm No. 11 of the Fatui Harbingers, codename Childe, but I also go by Tartaglia. And you... Hmm, you too like to cause quite the stir, don't you?"

Tartaglia is from Snezhnaya but moved to Liyue Harbor and works as a Fatui Harbinger.

While he does have a childlike exterior, on the inside he is a finely honed instrument of war.
