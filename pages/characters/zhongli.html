Zhongli

| | |

December 31st | Lapis Dei

BASIC INFO:

"A new contract? Okay. I'm still on leave, but I can accompany you for a while. What name should I use on the contract? I have a great many names, though when on leave... I tend to go by Zhongli."

Zhongli is a now retired Geo Archon of Liyue, and has ruled for 6000 years, and ruled by contracts.

After retiring as an Archon, he currently works alongside the 77th Director in the Wangshen Funeral Parlour. whom of which he 'cannot stand'.
