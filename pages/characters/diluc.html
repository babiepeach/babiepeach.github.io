Diluc

| | |

April 30th | Noctua

BASIC INFO:

"Diluc, of Mondstadt. Not interested in idle chit-chat. If you have things you want to get done, let me know."

Diluc is a bartender and is the owner of Mondstadt's largest wine producer, which has made him incredibly wealthy.

He used to work for the Knights of Favonius before his fathers demise, causing him to leave and loathe it, yet he does still respect some of the hardworking knights.
