Keqing

| | |

November 20th | Trulla Cementarii

BASIC INFO:

"I am Keqing, Yuheng of the Liyue Qixing. We live in an era of change, as the old order that has existed for a thousand years is about to be rewritten."

Keqing is the Yuheng of the Liyue Qixing, in Liyue, working alongside other higher-up members of the Qixing and the Adepti.

She is a work-a-holic, despite her young age, and views the Gods and Visions in a less-than-favourable light.
