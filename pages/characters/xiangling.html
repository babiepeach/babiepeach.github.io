"Hello, nice to meet you! My name is Xiangling, I'm a chef from Liyue! My favorite place is the chicken... I mean, the kitchen!"

While she does love cooking and everyone loves her cooking, people do easily shy away from her newest creations due to their extreme nature in ingredients.
