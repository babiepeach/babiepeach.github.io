Kaedehara Kazuha

| | |

October 29th | Acer Palmatum

BASIC INFO:

""Come driving rain or winds that churn, I shall return, by blade alone, armed, if barefoot, to my home..." I am Kaedehara Kazuha, a wanderer who roams the land."

Kaedehara Kazuha is from Inazuma, but works onlyside those of The Crux in Liyue, but has also made appearances in Mondstat.

He is one to enjoy poetry, namely Haikus, but doesn't often admit this although he is often inspired to write them.
