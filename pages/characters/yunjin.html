Yun Jin

May 21st | Opera Grandis

BASIC INFO:

"As the saying goes, you never truly know someone until you meet them in person, and at long last, I finally have the great honor of meeting you today. My name is Yun Jin, and I am humbled to be the director of of the Yun-Han Opera Troupe."

Yun Jin is the director for Yun-Han Opera Troupe in Liyue Harbour.

She is extremely talented at singing and dancing, so much so that whenever she performs, the Opera House is constantly packed for as long as she is there for.
