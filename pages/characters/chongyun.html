Chongyun

| | |

September 7th | Nubis Caesor

BASIC INFO:

"My name is Chongyun. My family has practiced the art of exorcism in Liyue for many generations. Fate has been generous in allowing my path to cross with that of your distinguished self."

Chongyun is an exorcist in Liyue, and is from a big family of exorcists, and is all Yang energy.

Due to his Yang energy, he has an influx of body temperature, causing him to need to eat a cold ice-pop often to regulate his temperature.
