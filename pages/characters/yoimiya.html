BASIC INFO:

"Welcome to Naganohara! My name is Yoimiya — just let me know if you need anything! We have Kushikatsu, Egg Roll... Wait, no, we're not a restaurant. We make fireworks!"

Yoimiya is the current owner of Naganohara Fireworks in Inazuma, and is the person everyone goes to for fireworks.

She is a reckless and unprepared person a lot of the time, but when it comes to fireworks she makes sure everything is done properly.
