Yae Miko

| | |

June 27th | Divina Vulpes

BASIC INFO:

"I am the Guuji of the Grand Narukami Shrine. The purpose of my visit is to monitor your every move, for such is the order of the shrine... Oh, come on, don't be so nervous."

Yae Miko is the head shrine maiden of the Grand Narukami Shine, and the editor-in-chief of the Yae Publishing House in Inazuma.

As formal as Yae Miko appears at first, when speaking with close friends, she is more blunt and cynical.
