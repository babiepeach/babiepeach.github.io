Thoma

| | |

January 9th | Rubeum Scutum

BASIC INFO:

"It's good to meet you at last, Traveler. I've been hearing about you for a long time now. I'm your new buddy, Thoma — as long as that's okay with you, of course!"

Thoma is the maid for the Yashiro Commission's Kamisato Clan in Inazuma.

He was originally from Mondstat, but moved to Inazuma to originally bring his father some wine, but fell overboard, making him poor.
