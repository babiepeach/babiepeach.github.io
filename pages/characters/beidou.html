BASIC INFO:

"I'm Beidou. You've heard of my ship, The Crux, and its crew? If you too love adventure, then join me. I've got your back."

Beidou is the Captain of the ship The Crux, alongside her crew, in Liyue Harbor.

She is loved by all and is highly valued by the powers of Liyue, even if some find it hard to show their appreciation.
