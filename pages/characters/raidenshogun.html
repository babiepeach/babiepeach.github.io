BASIC INFO:

"No salutations needed. My exalted status shall not be disclosed as we travel among the common folk. I acknowledge that you are a person of superior ability."

The Raiden Shogun is the puppet to the Electro Archon of Inazuma, Ei.

While she is a puppet, she is capable of immense strength matching Ei, due to Ei's rules for the puppet.
