Fischl

| | |

May 27th | Corvus

BASIC INFO:

"I, Fischl, Prinzessin der Verurteilung, descend upon this land by the call of fate an— Oh, you are also a traveler from another world? Very well, I grant you permission to travel with me."

Fischl is a member of Mondstat's Adventures Guild, and travels alongside her night raven companion Oz.

She appears to have created Oz with both her vision and her benevolent character, who also helps those interacting with Fischl to translate what she's saying.
