Mona

August 31st | Astrolabos

BASIC INFO:

"I am Astrologist Mona Megistus, meaning "The Great Astrologist Mona." If it is divination you seek from me, then I ask you respect my name by learning it wholly, here and now."

Mona is a young Astrologist who lives in Mondstat, usually alongside her master.

She is often quite poor, hungry and homeless as she carelessly spends all her Mora on Astrology equipment, rather than herself.
