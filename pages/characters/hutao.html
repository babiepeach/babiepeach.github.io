BASIC INFO:

"Yoh, now why might you be looking for me, hm? Oh, you didn't know? I'm the 77th Director of the Wangsheng Funeral Parlor, Hu Tao."

Hu Tao is from Liyue, and is the 77th director of the Wangsheng Funeral Parlor.

She often struggles to make friends due to her unusual nature and manner.

MORE INFO:

Hu Tao is the 77th Director of the Wangsheng Funeral Parlor, taking over presumably from her father, the 76th Director, or from her Grandfather, the 75th Director, who bore her the hat she wears today. She is also rather adept in the art of Poetry, and has shared this with Xingqiu. The Wangsheng Funeral Parlor can be estimated to being between 2000-5000 years old, as it was founded part-way through the Archon War to help rid of a plague bestowed upon Liyue by the Yaksha and the bodies it lay in its wake. Her Pyro vision is not often acknowledged by Hu Tao, however she appreciates it as a recognition of her strength and resilience.
