BASIC INFO:

"Spark Knight Klee of the Knights of Favonius, reporting for duty! ...There's some more, but uh, I forgot. I'm not so good at remembering..."

Klee works alongside the Knights of Favonius in Mondstat, holding the title of Spark Knight.

She, like most of the child characters in the game, has a poor memory.
